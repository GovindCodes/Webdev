About Us

We’re Corrie and Ty, a wife and husband duo with a passion for creativity. Photography is Corrie’s creative outlet of choice—she’s a published wedding and portrait photographer based in Oregon (though she’s known to travel the country).

One day after a weekend wedding excursion, while making sure everything was cleaned and put away properly, Corrie decided she was bored with the same old lens caps displaying the manufacturer’s name. “I’m being creative; why can’t my equipment be, too?!” Thus Lens Captions was born.
